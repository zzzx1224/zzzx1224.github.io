About me

I am a Ph.D. student in the MultiX lab at the University of Amsterdam supervised by Marcel Worring and Xiantong Zhen. Prior to that, I received my Master’s and Bachelor’s degree from Beihang University.

My current research focuses on: multi-task learning with limited data, Bayesian models and meta-learning.
