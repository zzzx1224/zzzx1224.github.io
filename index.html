About me

I am currently a last-year Ph.D. student in VIS Lab & AIM Lab at University van Amsterdam, supervised by Cees Snoek. I am also a member of ELLIS society. Before joining University van Amsterdam in 2020, I got my Master’s and Bachelor’s degree from Beihang University.

My current research focuses on: domain generalization, test-time adaptation, and meta-learning.
